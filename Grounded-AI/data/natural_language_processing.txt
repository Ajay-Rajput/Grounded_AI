Natural Language Processing (NLP) is a field of AI focused on enabling computers to understand and generate human language.

Key NLP tasks include:
- Text classification
- Named Entity Recognition (NER)
- Machine Translation
- Sentiment Analysis
- Question Answering

Transformers revolutionized NLP. The attention mechanism allows models to focus on important words in a sentence.

Popular NLP models:
- BERT
- GPT
- T5

Tokenization is the process of splitting text into smaller units called tokens.
